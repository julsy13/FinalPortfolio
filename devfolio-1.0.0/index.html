About me

Hi, I’m Julian Oliver B. Molina, a 4th year Information Technology student at Mapúa Malayan Colleges Laguna, with a strong interest in Cybersecurity and DevOps. I specialize in backend development and have hands-on experience with PHP (CodeIgniter), C#, Python, HTML/CSS, and mobile development.

I’ve also contributed to a doctor appointment system, where I focused on backend logic and system functionality, and have gained experience in mobile app development, giving me a broader understanding of full-stack and cross-platform development.
